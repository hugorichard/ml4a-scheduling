mlforscheduling.ucb_rr¶

mlforscheduling.ucb_rr(jobs, delta=0.001)¶

UCB with Round Robin like exploration.

Jobs of each types are divided into small pieces of length given by the discretization step. A UCB based algorithm is then run over each type, running a piece of the job of the chosen type. The UCB algorithm gets reward 1 if the running piece is the last before the job completes and 0 otherwise. We use the confidence bounds of the exponential distribution.

Parameters:
jobsnp array of size k, n

jobs[i, j] is the processing times of the jth job of type i

deltafloat

Discretization step
